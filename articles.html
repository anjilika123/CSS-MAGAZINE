Understanding Digital Citizenship

Digital citizenship refers to the responsible and ethical use of technology. It promotes respectful communication, privacy awareness, and critical thinking in digital spaces. Students must understand that their actions online have consequences. Practicing proper digital behavior ensures a safer and more positive environment for everyone.

Key Highlights

  • Think Before You Post
  • Protect Personal Information
  • Report Cyberbullying
  • Respect Others Online
