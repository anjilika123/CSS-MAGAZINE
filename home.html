Editor's Note

Digital citizenship is more than just using technology. It is about using the internet responsibly, respectfully, and safely. In today’s digital age, students spend most of their time online for learning, communication, and entertainment.

Understanding digital rights, responsibilities, and online ethics helps create a safer and more positive digital community. Every click, post, and comment matters.
